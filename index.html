Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs.

    • The table below shows nutritional values per serving without the additional fillings.


    Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs:

      In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of milk or water for a fluffier texture.

    2. Heat the pan:

      Place a non-sticky frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

    3. Cook the omelette:

      Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

    4. Add fillings(Optional):

      When the eggs begin to set at the edges but one still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings on one half of the omelette.

    5. Fold and serve:

      As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filings.Let it cook for another minute and slide it onto a plate.

    6. Enjoy:

      Serve hot with additional salt or pepper if needed.
